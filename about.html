About UniNest

What is UniNest?

UniNest is a tailored roommate finder platform built for students at Kathmandu University. It aims to simplify the process of finding roommates or rooms by offering personalized matching, clean UI, and seamless interaction between room seekers and providers.

Built with purpose and empathy, UniNest addresses the real needs of students: affordability, compatibility, and trust.
